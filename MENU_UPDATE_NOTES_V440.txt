Langar Bar V4.4.0 Breakfast Menu Update

What changed:
1. Added Breakfast category with egg/omelette items and breakfast combos.
2. Added Breakfast Add-ons & Upgrades.
3. Every breakfast item/combo includes one drink choice: orange juice 200ml, espresso, Americano or classic tea.
4. Added cappuccino and latte breakfast upgrades.
5. Updated taco prices to €7.90/€8.00.
6. Updated tapas to Duo/Trio/Quartet model.
7. Updated focaccia pizza prices.
8. Updated dessert and soft serve pricing.
9. Added Extra Sauces category.
10. Bumped localStorage menu key to langar_menu_v7 and service worker cache to V4.4.0.

Supabase/cloud menu:
- Option 1: Run langar_bar_menu_seed_v44_breakfast_update.sql in Supabase SQL Editor.
- Option 2: Login in Admin and upload/sync the local menu. The admin-cloud seed now attempts to deactivate old menu items/categories first.

Note:
Sandwich prices are still kept from the existing app because final sandwich selling prices were not confirmed in the normatives. Descriptions now state that those prices are temporary until approved.
