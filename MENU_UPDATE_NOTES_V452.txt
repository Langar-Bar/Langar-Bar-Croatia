Langar Bar V4.5.2 — Order ETA + Stronger Admin Alarm

- Admin Orders can send estimated ready time to customer: 10, 15, 20, 30, 45, 60, 90 minutes.
- Optional customer message can be saved with the order.
- Customer app shows order status plus estimated ready time in My recent orders.
- Pickup/delivery customers can receive in-app/browser notifications when status or estimated time changes, while the app/PWA is active.
- Admin tablet alarm now uses a stronger square-wave pattern and progressively increases volume until the order is checked/accepted.
- New SQL: langar_bar_v452_order_eta_alarm_fix.sql

Important: browser audio still depends on device speaker volume and requires tapping Enable alarm sound once on the admin tablet.
