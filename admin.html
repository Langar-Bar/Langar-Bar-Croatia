Settings / Rules

Referral rule: reward is created only after the invited member registers, places a paid online order, and the order is completed. Default reward: €0.50 Langar Credit.

Birthday rule: one-time digital card: 40% for the birthday guest and 20% for up to 3 friends.

Welcome rule: new Langar Club member receives one-time Free Espresso Card.

Remaris-ready fields: pos_provider, remaris_item_id, remaris_category_id, receipt_number, remaris_receipt_id, sync_status.

Alcohol: 18+ warning active; alcoholic items are not orderable online by default.

Receipt: if fiscalized receipt is not issued, customer is not obliged to pay.
