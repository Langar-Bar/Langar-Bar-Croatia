Langar Bar V4.5.8 — Account Sync + Tapas + Ice Cream Fix

Changes:
- My recent orders is now account-authoritative when the customer is logged in.
- Logged-in orders are stored in account-specific local cache: langar_orders_user_<auth_uid>.
- Guest/device orders are separated from member/account orders.
- Cloud sync replaces the account order view with Cloud orders so one account shows the same order history on phone, tablet and computer.
- Older guest/token orders on the device are claimed to the logged-in account when possible.
- SQL get_my_customer_orders now claims older orders with the same profile phone number into the logged-in account.
- Tapas category is forced to the full correct menu even if the Cloud menu has zero-price/old Tapas rows.
- Tapas Duo/Trio/Quartet now open a flavor chooser in Order: Hummus, Eggplant, Tuna, Guacamole, Chicken and Red Bean.
- Focaccia is now a Tapas upgrade option inside the item, not a standalone order item.
- Ice Cream is now a separate category with its own cone icon.
- Soft serve ice cream items were moved out of Desserts into Ice Cream.
